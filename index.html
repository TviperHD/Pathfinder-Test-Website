Pathfinder

About Pathfinder

Pathfinder is a character from the video game Apex Legends. Pathfinder is a recon character that uses his grapple hook to reach higher places. He also has a zipline that him and his teammates can use.

All 8 original characters

Apex Legends came out with 8 original characters each with there own abilities and personalities.

Bangalore

Taking on the role of Professional Soldier, Bangalore is your go-to weapons expert when you want to rack up some damage on your opponents.

Bloodhound

Assuming the role of Technological Tracker, Bloodhound is your go-to tracking expert when wanting to hunt down enemy opponents in Apex Legends.

Caustic

Taking on the role of Toxic Trapper, Caustic uses his scientistic background to trap enemy opponents in a range of toxic gases.

Gibraltar

Claiming the role of Shielded Fortress, Gibraltar's unique shielding abilities make him a valuable defensive member to any squad in Apex Legends.

Lifeline

Claiming one of the most important roles in the game, Lifeline's experience as a Combat Medic could very well be your teams saving grace in Apex Legends.

Mirage

Mirage is Apex Legend's Holographic Trickster, as his sneaky cloaking ability and vanishing act will leave your opponents frustrated and confused.

Pathfinder

Claiming the role as Forward Scout, Pathfinder's surveying skill set and grappling abilities make traversing the map a breeze.

Wraith

Taking on the role of Inter-dimensional Skirmisher, Wraith's ability to travel between voids will give you the upper hand when going against enemy opponents in close quarter combat.
